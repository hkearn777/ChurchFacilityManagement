Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Church Facility Management ("we", "our", or "us") is a maintenance request management application for internal church facility operations. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

Our application collects and stores:

  • Maintenance Request Data: Descriptions, requestor names, dates, building information, and status updates
  • Images: Photos uploaded as part of maintenance requests are stored in Dropbox
  • Contact Information: Email addresses for notification purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Managing and tracking facility maintenance requests
  • Sending email notifications about request status changes
  • Storing maintenance photos and documentation
  • Generating reports for facility management

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sheets: For storing maintenance request data
  • Dropbox: For storing uploaded images and attachments
  • Gmail: For sending email notifications

Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data. We only share information necessary for the operation of the application.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections
  • Access to the application is controlled through authentication
  • API credentials are stored securely and never exposed to end users

6. Data Retention

Maintenance request data is retained as long as needed for facility management purposes. Completed requests may be archived but not deleted to maintain historical records.

7. Your Rights

As an internal church application, you have the right to:

  • Access your submitted maintenance requests
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to record-keeping requirements)

8. Children's Privacy

This application is intended for internal church staff and authorized users only. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
